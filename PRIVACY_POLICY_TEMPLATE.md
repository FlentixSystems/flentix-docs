# PRIVACY POLICY — TEMPLATE

> **⚠️ TEMPLATE — NOT YET LEGALLY REVIEWED.** This is a drafting template for the
> Flentix ecosystem, written to GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) and Spanish
> LOPDGDD (Ley Orgánica 3/2018) requirements. It **must be reviewed and finalised
> by a qualified Spanish data-protection lawyer** before any hub publishes it.
> Each `[BRACKETED]` field is completed per hub at onboarding. Not legal advice.

---

## How to use this template

- Each **hub (operator)** is the **data Controller** and adopts this policy as their
  own, completing the `[BRACKETED]` fields with their details.
- **Medacrii Associates Ltd** is named as the **Processor** (the platform operator
  handling data on the hub's behalf).
- Publish in **Spanish** as the primary version (legally required clarity for Spanish
  data subjects); an English version may be offered alongside. Keep both in sync.
- Where a hub does **not** offer a given feature (e.g. the community module), delete
  the relevant section.

## 2. What this policy covers

This policy explains what personal data we collect when you book or use our
workspace, virtual office, or community features, why we collect it, the legal basis
for doing so, how long we keep it, who it is shared with, and the rights you have
over it.

## 3. The personal data we collect

**a) When you book or become a member**
- Identity and contact details: name, email address, telephone number, billing
  address.
- Booking and membership details: spaces/services booked, dates, attendance /
  check-in records, membership status and history.
- Payment-related data: processed securely via **Stripe**; we do not store full card
  numbers. (Stripe acts as an independent controller/processor for payment data
  under its own terms.)
- For virtual-office and telephony services, where applicable: identity verification
  documents (ID/passport) and proof of address, collected to meet Spanish regulatory
  and telephony-compliance requirements.
- Metadata of received physical correspondence handled on your behalf: sender, date
  received, envelope photography, scans where you request them, and processing/
  forwarding history.

**b) When you use the community features** *(delete if the Hub does not offer this)*
- Account/identity: name and email from your chosen sign-in (Google, Apple, or
  email/password) and the associated provider identifier.
- Profile data you choose to provide: photo, job title, company, professional bio,
  skills, links (e.g. LinkedIn/website), and availability indicators (e.g. "open to
  work").
- Activity: posts, events, RSVPs, reactions, "interested" signals, and last-active /
  attendance signals used to keep the directory current.

**c) Automatically**
- Technical data: log-in session data, device/browser information, and essential
  cookies needed to operate the service securely (see our Cookie Policy / §11).

## 4. Why we use your data, and our legal basis (GDPR Art. 6)

| Purpose | Legal basis |
|---|---|
| Providing and managing your booking, membership, and workspace access | **Contract** (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Processing payments and issuing invoices | **Contract** / **Legal obligation** (Art. 6(1)(b),(c)) |
| Identity/address verification for virtual office & telephony | **Legal obligation** (Art. 6(1)(c)) |
| Anti-money-laundering checks and record-keeping for registered-address and virtual-office services | **Legal obligation** (Art. 6(1)(c)) — Spanish Ley 10/2010 de Prevención del Blanqueo de Capitales |
| Operating the community directory and feed, keeping it current | **Legitimate interests** (Art. 6(1)(f)) — running a members' community — *and your consent* for your public profile visibility (see §5) |
| Showing your profile publicly in the member directory | **Consent** (Art. 6(1)(a)) — you control your visibility and can withdraw it at any time |
| Sending you marketing, newsletters, events and offers | **Consent** (Art. 6(1)(a)) — opt-in only, withdrawable at any time |
| Security, fraud prevention, and keeping the service running | **Legitimate interests** (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| Complying with legal, tax, and accounting obligations | **Legal obligation** (Art. 6(1)(c)) |

Where we rely on **legitimate interests**, we have assessed that our interest in
running the service does not override your rights and freedoms. You may object — see
§8.

## 5. Consent, and how to withdraw it

Some processing relies on your **consent**, which is always **optional, specific, and
freely given**:
- **Marketing/newsletter:** opt-in only; never pre-ticked. You can withdraw at any
  time via the unsubscribe link in any message or by contacting us.
- **Community profile visibility:** you choose whether your profile appears in the
  member directory, and at what scope (hidden / visible within this hub / — where
  offered — visible across the wider Flentix network). The wider-network option is
  **off unless you actively opt in**. You can change or withdraw this at any time in
  your profile settings.

Withdrawing consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before
withdrawal, nor processing under another legal basis (e.g. running your booking).

## 6. Who we share your data with

- **Medacrii Associates Ltd** — our platform processor (United Kingdom), under a Data
  Processing Agreement.
- **Service providers (sub-processors)** acting on documented instructions, including:
  Supabase (EU-hosted database/infrastructure), Stripe (payments), Brevo
  (transactional and, where you've consented, marketing email), and — for telephony
  services where applicable — our telecommunications provider.
- **Other members:** if you choose to make your profile visible, the profile
  information you provide is visible to other members of this hub (and, only if you
  opt in, the wider Flentix network). **Your contact details and verification
  documents are never shown to other members.**
- **Authorities or third parties** where required by law, or to establish, exercise,
  or defend legal claims.

We do **not** sell your personal data, and we do not allow it to be used for
third-party advertising.

## 7. International transfers

Your data is stored within the **European Union** (Supabase, EU region). Some
processors (e.g. Medacrii in the United Kingdom, or Stripe) may process data outside
the EU. Where this happens, transfers are protected by an **adequacy decision** (the
UK benefits from an EU adequacy decision) or by **Standard Contractual Clauses**
and appropriate safeguards as required by Chapter V of the GDPR.

## 8. Your rights

Under the GDPR and Spanish LOPDGDD, you have the right to:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Rectify** inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten"), including your community profile and
  posts, subject to legal retention obligations.
- **Restrict** or **object to** processing based on legitimate interests, including
  direct marketing (which we will always stop on request).
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a structured, commonly used,
  machine-readable format.
- **Withdraw consent** at any time, where processing is based on consent.
- **Not be subject** to solely automated decisions producing legal/significant
  effects (we do not carry out such decision-making).

To exercise any right, contact **[HUB PRIVACY EMAIL]**. We will respond within **one
month** (extendable by two further months for complex requests, with notice). There
is normally no charge.

**Complaints:** if you believe we have not handled your data properly, you may lodge
a complaint with the Spanish supervisory authority, the **Agencia Española de
Protección de Datos (AEPD)** — www.aepd.es, C/ Jorge Juan 6, 28001 Madrid — or with
the supervisory authority in your EU country of residence. We'd appreciate the chance
to resolve it first.

## 9. How long we keep your data

We keep your data only as long as necessary for the purposes above:
- **Booking, membership and financial records:** for the duration of your
  relationship with us and thereafter as required by Spanish tax and commercial law
  (generally up to [6] years for accounting records).
- **Community profile and posts:** while you are an active member. If you become
  inactive, your profile may be hidden from the directory and later archived; it is
  retained only as long as needed and is deleted on request or after our defined
  retention period.
- **Marketing data:** until you withdraw consent or after a period of inactivity.
- **Verification documents:** only as long as required to meet the relevant
  regulatory obligation, then securely deleted.
- **Correspondence metadata (mail handling):** retained for the duration of your
  virtual-office service and for [a defined period] thereafter, then deleted, unless
  a longer period is required by law.
- **Anti-money-laundering records:** retained for the period required by Spanish Ley
  10/2010 (generally [up to 10] years from the end of the business relationship),
  after which they are securely deleted.

## 10. How we protect your data

Data is hosted on EU infrastructure with access controls, encryption in transit,
row-level security isolating each hub's data, and role-based access. Medacrii applies
technical and organisational measures appropriate to the risk, and is bound by
confidentiality and the Data Processing Agreement.

## 11. Cookies

We use **essential cookies** required to operate the service securely (e.g. managing
your login session, security tokens, and payment transactions). With your consent we
may also use optional **analytical cookies** to improve the service. See our [Cookie
Policy] or manage your preferences via the cookie banner.

## 12. Children

Our services are intended for adults (and members aged 18+). We do not knowingly
collect data from children. If you believe a minor has provided us data, contact us
and we will delete it.
